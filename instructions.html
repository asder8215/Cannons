Context

Welcome to Cannons! This game is inspired by another game called Brickbreaker. However, the differences is that the cannon cannot be manually moved; it automatically moves in pendulum motion.

Controls + Goals

That being said, the only button that you need to press for this game is the spacebar. The goal is to eliminate all the balls on the level with the limited ammos you have on your cannon.

Useful Information

Light blue balls give 100 points, purple balls give 300 points, and yellow balls give 3+ ammos.

Last Message...

I hope you enjoy the game!
